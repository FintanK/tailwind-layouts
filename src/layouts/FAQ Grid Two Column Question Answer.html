Answers to Your Questions

Find quick answers to frequently asked questions about our platform and services.

How secure is my data?
We employ industry-standard security protocols, including end-to-end encryption and regular audits, to ensure your data is safe and protected.
Can I integrate with other tools?
Yes! Our platform offers extensive integration capabilities with popular services like Slack, Jira, Google Workspace, and many others via Zapier or our developer API.
What kind of support do you offer?
We provide comprehensive support through our knowledge base, email support, and live chat during business hours. Enterprise plans include dedicated account managers.
Is there a mobile app available?
Currently, we offer a fully responsive web application accessible on all devices. Native mobile apps for iOS and Android are on our development roadmap.
How often are new features released?
We follow an agile development process and typically release updates and new features every 2-4 weeks, along with regular maintenance.
What happens if I exceed my plan limits?
You will be notified when approaching your plan limits. You can easily upgrade your plan at any time through your account settings to accommodate growth.
