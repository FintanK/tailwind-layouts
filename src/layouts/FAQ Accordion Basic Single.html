Frequently Asked Questions

We offer a 30-day return policy for most items. Please visit our returns page for detailed information and instructions on how to initiate a return. Some exclusions apply.

Standard shipping typically takes 3-5 business days within the continental US. Expedited options are available at checkout. International shipping times vary.

Unfortunately, once an order is placed, we cannot modify it. If you need to make changes, please cancel the order (if possible) and place a new one, or contact customer support for assistance.
