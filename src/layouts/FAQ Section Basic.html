Frequently asked questions

What's the best way to get started?

The best way to start is by signing up for our free trial. This gives you full access to the platform's core features for 14 days, allowing you to explore and see if it fits your needs without any commitment.

Do you offer discounts for non-profits?

Yes, we offer special discounts for registered non-profit organizations. Please contact our sales team with your organization's details for more information on eligibility and pricing.

How does billing work?

We offer both monthly and annual billing options. Annual plans come with a discount. You can manage your subscription and view invoices directly from your account settings.

Can I change my plan later?

Absolutely! You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes will be reflected in your next billing cycle. Contact support if you need assistance.
